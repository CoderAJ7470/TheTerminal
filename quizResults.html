So, what is "YS Flight Simulator?"

YS Flight Simulator is a completely free, awesome flight simulation game (and then some). That is right, completely free. Since the first release in 1999, it has undergone multiple improvements over the past decade (and more!). With a large online community at YS Flight Headquarters (YSFHQ), as well as a large Japanese community and some branch forums, there are literally hundreds of addons in the form of packs and individual releases.

You can fly offline, or online on servers. You can fly civilian and commercial planes from small props to airliners, or do combat in highly detailed fighters and bombers, or if you fancy, get in an aerobatic plane like this Extra 300 and do some fancy maneuvers! Addons range from small and large jets to wonderful sceneries and maps, to highly detailed air force aircraft, aircraft carriers, weapons etc. Or maybe aircraft are not what you are after and you're more into cars, spaceships or maybe even Asimo?

Now at first glance, the graphics of the sceneries might seem old-school and not that impressive, which is understandable especially for someone used to the latest graphics renderings, but keep in mind, this simulator has been tuned to be compatible for older computers as well. That being said, the coming 2014 release does show the inclusion of image-based ground texturing. To be completely honest though, I find the flexibility and simplicity of the game, coupled with the vast array of addons and packs (and did I mention it's free?) basically eliminate the "graphics" deal (for lack of a better expression). I could go on and on, but I recommend you start at the game author's website, where you'll find not only the game itself but other creations, including a new 3D modelling program in which you can create aircraft.
